Cold brew sausages water

Refreshing drink perfect for hot summer days

Step by step guide:

  1. Take you favorite sausages out of the packaging.
  2. Put sausages into the cold water.
  3. Stir clockwise with spoon 10 times.
  4. Taste the water to see if it has right aroma - If it's too weak repeat step no. 3. If it's too strong add some water. If perfect proceed to the next step
  5. Pour the extract to the cup. Serve chilled with favorite additives.

Tip: To avoid waste of sausages, eat them afterwards.
