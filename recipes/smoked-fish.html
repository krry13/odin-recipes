Smoked fish without head

Perfect protein source

Ingredients:

  • Supermarket-grade ready to eat smoked fish

Step by step guide:

  1. Check the freshness of fish smoked in the store.
  2. If it seems they haven't been on the shelf for more than a week than buy smoked fish from your local supermarket.
  3. Unpack the fish and consume without fish bones.

Tip: Unfortunately stretch foil is not edible.
